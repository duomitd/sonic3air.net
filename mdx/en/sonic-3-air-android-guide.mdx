# How to Install Sonic 3 A.I.R. on Android: Step-by-Step Guide

**Meta Description:**  
Learn how to install Sonic 3 A.I.R. on your Android device with this comprehensive guide. Find out how to get the Sonic 3 & Knuckles ROM, install the APK, and start playing the game.

---

## Step 1: Prepare the ROM File

To get started, you need a compatible **Sonic 3 & Knuckles ROM**. The recommended version is the one from Steam.

1. **Download the Steam ROM:**  
   If you own Sonic 3 & Knuckles on Steam, you can extract the ROM directly from your Steam installation. Otherwise, you can download the ROM from [here](https://archive.org/download/sonic-knuckles-w-sonic-3/Sonic_Knuckles_wSonic3.bin).

   
---

## Step 2: Download the Sonic 3 A.I.R. APK

Next, you'll need to download the **Sonic 3 A.I.R. APK**. Make sure to download it from a trusted source to avoid malware.

- **Download the APK:**  
  Get the latest APK version from [this link](https://github.com/Eukaryot/sonic3air/releases/download/v24.02.02.0-stable/sonic3air_game.apk).

  The latest version of the APK is approximately 44.87MB and was released on July 27, 2024.


---

## Step 3: Install the APK on Your Android Device

Before installing the APK, you may need to enable installation from **unknown sources** on your Android device.

1. Go to **Settings > Security** and enable **Install from Unknown Sources**.
2. Once this is enabled, open the **APK file** you downloaded, and follow the on-screen instructions to install the game.

## Step 4: Transfer the ROM File to Your Android Device

Now you need to transfer the **Sonic 3 & Knuckles ROM** to your Android device. You can do this in several ways:

- **Via USB cable:** Connect your device to your computer and copy the ROM file to any folder on your Android device (e.g., Downloads folder).
- **Via Google Drive:** If you prefer, upload the ROM file to Google Drive and access it on your Android device.

   <img src="steam-rom.png" alt="Sonic 3 A.I.R. steam rom" width="300" height="200" />

---

## Step 5: Use ZArchiver to Manage Files (Optional)

If you'd like to add **MODs** to the game, you can use **ZArchiver**. This app helps you manage and unzip any files, including game mods.

1. Download **ZArchiver** from the Google Play Store.
2. Open ZArchiver and navigate to the ROM folder to manage or extract any MOD files you want to use.

---

## Step 6: Start the Game

Once the APK is installed and the ROM file is transferred to your Android device:

1. Open the **Sonic 3 A.I.R. app**.
2. The game will prompt you to select the **Sonic 3 & Knuckles ROM** file. Navigate to where you saved the ROM and select it.
3. The game will start, and you’re ready to enjoy Sonic 3 A.I.R. on your Android device!
